Hello!

I’m a reality tv editor turned programmer. I just wrapped up my training at Flatiron School and I’m excited to combine my new software engineering skills with my years of experience interfacing with clients, piecing together puzzles, and clarifying chaos to build technology that will help real people simplify the chaos of their own lives.

For some people, technology is a lifestyle. But for me, it’s functional. It’s useful. It’s about lessening my mental load or remembering something for some unknown time in the future. It’s about removing or simplifying boring, frustrating, or unwanted tasks from my life to give me with more free time to do something I actually enjoy...spending with my family, going for a run, working on a hobby. I love development because it allows me to create or improve on something that is a helping hand which in turn saves people time and frustration -- making lives a little lighter than they otherwise would have been.

Visit my blog for to learn more about me and my coding journey.
